
|Input size | Required Time Complexity|
|---------- | ------------------------|
|$n <= 10$    | $O(n!)$
|	$n <= 20$    | $O(2^n)$
|	$n <= 500$   | $O(n^3)$
|	$n <= 5000$  | $O(n^2)$
|	$n <= 10^6$  | $O(nlogn)$ or $O(n)$
|	$n$ is large | $O(1)$ or $O(logn)$
